Privacy Policy

At Clyvaro Solutions, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to our website. You may browse our pages freely without providing any data.

2. Contact Forms

If you choose to contact us through our contact form, we may receive your name, email address, or message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

3. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect visitor data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share or sell your information to third parties. If we use third-party services (such as email or hosting providers), they will only have access to information necessary to perform their functions.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you provide. However, please note that no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
